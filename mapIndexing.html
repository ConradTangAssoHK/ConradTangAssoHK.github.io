Hong Kong Map Indexing


Note: Below introduces the basic indexing system of paper maps in Hong Kong.

Link to Hong Kong Maps indexing calculator: Click here


Paper maps covering Hong Kong are indexed by a 4 field system depending on their scale:

  • 1:20,000 topographic maps - 16 sheets
  • (HM20C color series, and HM20C/2 black and white series)
  • 1:10,000 derived plans - 59 sheets (as at June 2014)
  • (SM10C series generalized from HP1C maps)
  • 1:5,000 derived plans - 167 sheets (as at February 2015)
  • (HP5C series generalized from HP1C maps)
  • 1:1,000 basic plans - 3266 sheets (as at October 2014)
  • (HP1C maps)

Deriving grids

  • HM20C 1:20,000 topographics maps
  • printed on sheets with size 685 × 980 mm, presenting area of 12,000 × 15,000 m
  • SM10C 1:10,000 derived maps
  • printed on sheets with size 685 × 980 mm, presenting area of 6,000 × 7,500 m
  • HP5C 1:5,000 derived maps
  • printed on sheets with size 750 × 850 mm, presenting area of 1,200 × 1,500 m
  • HP1C 1:1,000 basic plans
  • printed on sheets with size 750 × 850 mm, presenting area of 600 × 750 m


Example - Retriving map grid index with coordinates

Northing : 832,900m
Easting : 824,700m

First, we ignore the HK80 prefixes, i.e.
N 832,900 - 800,000 = 32,900m
E 824,900 - 800,000 = 24,900m

At HM20C level,
N 32,900 / 12,000 = 2.742 → 3
E 24,900 / 15,000 = 1.660 → 2
Grid(3,2) → 6 (Figure 3)


Then we retrieve the lower left corner (LLC) coordinates of grid 6, i.e.
(LLC) N 824,000 → 24,000
(LLC) E 815,000 → 15,000

And minus (N 32,900, E 24,700) by the LLC coordinates
N 32,900 - 24,000 = 8,900m
E 24,700 - 15,000 = 9,700m

At SM10C level,
N 8,900 / 6,000 = 1.4833 → 2
E 9,700 / 7,500 = 1.2933 → 2
Grid(2,2) → NE
i.e. 6-NE

LLC coordinates of grid 6-NE, i.e.
N 830,000 → 30,000m
E 822,500 → 22,500m

Minus
N 32,900 - 30,000 = 2,900m
E 24,700 - 22,500 = 2,200m

AT HP5C level,
N 2,900 / 1,200 = 2.416 → 3
E 2,200 / 1,500 = 1.467 → 2
Grid(3,2) → 12
i.e. 6-NE-12

LLC coordinates of grid 6-NE-12, i.e.
N 832,400 → 32,400m
E 824,000 → 24,000m

Minus
N 32,900 - 32,400 = 500m
E 24,700 - 24,000 = 700m

At HP1C level,
N 500 / 600 = 0.833 → 1
E 700 / 750 = 0.933 → 1
Grid(1,1) → C
i.e. 6-NE-12C


The upper left corner coordinates of grid 6-NE-12C is (833,000mN,824,000mE), referring to the coordinates used in this example (832,900mN, 824,700mE), this point is within the grid.
